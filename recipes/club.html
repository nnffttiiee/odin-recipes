ClubHouse

Description:

Do you enjoy a delicious ClubHouse? Who am I kidding, who doesn't!

I've scoured the web in search for a quick, easy and above all, tasty ClubHouse recipe and I've found one you and the entire family will love!

    Ingredients:

  • Turkey
  • Bacon
  • Ham
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mayo
  • White Bread
