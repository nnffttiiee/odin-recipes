Wings

Description:

Do you enjoy delicious Wings? Who am I kidding, who doesn't!

I've scoured the web in search for a quick, easy and above all, tasty wing recipe and I've found one you and the entire family will love!

    Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Paprika
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter

    Steps:

  1. Line a baking sheet with foil and dot it with butter.
  2. Combine flour, paprika, garlic powder, and salt & pepper in a mixing bowl.
  3. Fully coat each wing with the seasoning mix and place it on the baking sheet.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes at 425Â°F. Then turn the wings over and bake another 15 minutes.
